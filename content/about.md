Hello there! 

Thanks for taking an interest in learning more about me, I do hope the material you find on this site was worth the trip and this is just a quick snippet about myself so you might better understand the blogger behind the words. I am a second generation Puerto Rican who grew in up in the countryside of Georgia about an hour and a half south of Atlanta. Growing up I loved books, writing, Star Trek, and being in the Boy Scouts of America.

Upon applying to college, I was rejected out of all my first picks because I didn't apply myself and I am terrible at test taking. I was accepted to Southern Polytechnic State University and decided to major in Computer Science where I loved every minute of it. I made lifelong friends and in Masters program, I found the love of my life.

Our banner year had the following things happen in order: Wife got her Masters, We got engaged, I got my Masters, We started new jobs with the same company and we got Married. The years have gone by but they have been some of the happiest of my life. I now work as a principal architect consultant where I gotten the feeling of being college all over again: each client seems like a brand new course of study and some are more enjoyable than others. Software Development challenges my brain and it feels amazing to be challenged.
